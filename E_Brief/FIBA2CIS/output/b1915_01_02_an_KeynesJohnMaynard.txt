Dear Keynes,
I've got the letter you wrote to me in September. The money will be sent to the registry as soon as the war will be over. Please give my love to Johnson whom I appreciate more & more the longer I haven't seen him.
If you get this please write to me to the above address via the red cross Switzerland.
Yours
L. Wittgenstein
