Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an J. T. Saunders, 21. 7. 1942

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness.  Publiziert (WiC, S. 352). 

Text: 
Nuffield House 
Guy’s Hospital, S.E.I. 
21.7.42. 
Dear Saunders,
 I have now completely recovered from my illness & returned to my work at Guy’s Hospital. I’m afraid I don’t know whether I should have again applied for leave of absence. Would you be kind enough to let me know what steps, if any, I ought to take?
 Yours sincerely, 
L. Wittgenstein
