Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [26. 4. 1938]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 210; Cambridge Letters, S. 297). 

Text: 
81 East Rd
Tuesday
Dear Moore,
I find that I shall have to be in Paris on Thursday (day after tomorrow) so my Friday discussion is off. Would you please be so kind to let your pupils know this, & also that I shall lecture on Monday next, D.v..
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
