Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an John Maynard Keynes, [23. 10. 1913]

sourceDesc:  Original an der King's College Library in Cambridge; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 36; Cambridge Letters, S. 44). 

Text: 
J. M. Keynes Esq
Kings College
Cambridge
England
[Poststempel: SKJOLDEN, 23.X.13]
Dear Keynes, My address is: L. W. c/o Halvard Draegni Skiolden, Sogn, Norway. I did not go as far north as I thought I would. This is a splendid place.
Yours ever
L. Wittgenstein
