Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an J. T. Saunders, 23. 3. 1944

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness.  Publiziert (WiC, S. 362). 

Text: 
c/o Mrs Mann 
10 Langland Rd 
Mumbles 
Swansea 
23.3.44 
Dear Saunders,
Thank you for your letter, dated 18 March. I am grateful to the General Board for condoning my absence from Cambridge this term & for dispensing me from discharging my duties at the University next week.
Yours sincerely, 
L. Wittgenstein
