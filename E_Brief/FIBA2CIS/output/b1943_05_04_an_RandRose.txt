Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Rose Rand, 4. 5. [1943]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness. 

Text: 
28, Brandling Park
Newcastle
2 4.5. 
Dear Miss Rand,
Thanks for your letter & the chocolate. Please don’t send me presents; I’ld really rather not get them, & you know it. Don’t force me to send them back to you. I very much hope you’ll find some suitable work & somehow come to rest.
Good wishes! 
Yours sincerely 
L. Wittgenstein
