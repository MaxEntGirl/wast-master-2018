Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Bertrand Russell, 9. 2. 1919

sourceDesc:  Original in The Bertrand Russell Archives der McMaster University; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (Letters, S. 66; deutsch: Briefe, S. 84; Cambridge Letters, S. 107; WiC, S. 87). 

Text: 
Ludwig Wittgenstein
Leutnant i. d. Res.
Cassino
Provincia Caserta
Inglese
The Hon. Bertrand Russell
c/o Dr A.N. Whitehead
University College
London
Inghilterra
Dear Russell, I don't know your precise address but hope these lines will reach you somehow. I am prisoner in Italy since November and hope I may communicate with you after a three years interuption. I have done lots of logikal work which I am dying to let you know before publishing it. Ever yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
9.2.19.
