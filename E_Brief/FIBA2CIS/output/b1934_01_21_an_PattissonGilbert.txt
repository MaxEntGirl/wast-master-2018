Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Gilbert Pattisson, [nach 21. 1. 1934]

sourceDesc:  Original an der Trinity College Library; Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
 Zeichnung  
Monday
Dear Gilbert,
I've just been to the bank & given them the Cheque for Drury. Thanks awfully. ‒ 
Thanks also for your latest poem. I will make the same use of it as I would of any other poem of yours. ‒ 
I hear you say: "Did you say anything?" ‒ 
Yours b......y
Ludwig
