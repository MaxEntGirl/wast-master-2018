Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Walter Morley Fletcher, 21. 1. 1913

sourceDesc:  Original an der Trinity College Library; Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
21.1.13.
IV. ALLEEGASSE 16.
Wien
Dear Dr Fletcher,
My father died yesterday. I will leave Vienna on Saturday 25th & shall get to Cambridge either on Sunday night or Monday morning.
Yours sincerely
Ludwig Wittgenstein
