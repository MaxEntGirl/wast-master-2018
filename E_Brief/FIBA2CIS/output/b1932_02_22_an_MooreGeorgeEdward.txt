Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [22. 2. 1932]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 185; Cambridge Letters, S. 253). 

Text: 
Monday
Dear Moore,
I cann't come to tea with you tomorrow as I go to London to see the French Exhibition. Some people are taking me there in their car. I look forward hearing you on Friday. I'm sorry I wasn't well enough to hold my class last Friday.
Yours ever
Ludwig Wittgenstein
