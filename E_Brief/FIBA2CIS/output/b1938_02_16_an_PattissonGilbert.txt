Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Gilbert Pattisson, [16. 2. 1938]

sourceDesc:  Original an der Trinity College Library; Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
G. R. Pattisson Esq
42 Argyll Rd.
Kensington
London W.8.
[Poststempel: BAILE ATHA CLIATH, 16 FEB 1938]
Dear Gilbert, If I remember rightly this Cathedral was built, partly at least, by the Normans. Of course it's a long time ago & my memory isn't what it was then. My address is as follows:
36 Chelmsford Rd.
Ranelagh, Dublin.
I may need some money from you before long. So long! B....ly
Ludwig
