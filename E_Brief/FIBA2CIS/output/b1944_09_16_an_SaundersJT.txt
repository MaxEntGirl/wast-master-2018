Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an J. T. Saunders, 16. 9. 1944

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness.  Publiziert (WiC, S. 364). 

Text: 
c/o Rhees 
96 Bryn Rd 
Swansea 
16.9.44 
Dear Saunders,
Under the present circumstances it does not seem to make sense for me to take up war work again at the beginning of October. I shall, therefore, return to Cambridge instead, & resume my duties there at the beginning of Michaelmas Term.
Yours sincerely 
L. Wittgenstein
