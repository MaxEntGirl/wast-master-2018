Siglum: b1940_01_31_an_SraffaPiero

Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Piero Sraffa, 31. 1. 1939

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness, Kopie in BA. 

Text: 
Dear Sraffa,
It may interest you to read the enclosed letter. Ryle is very well informed. You see how correct my ideas were. Please don’t destroy Ryle’s letter.
Yours 
Ludwig Wittgenstein
