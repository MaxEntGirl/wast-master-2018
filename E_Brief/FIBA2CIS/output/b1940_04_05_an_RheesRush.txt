Text: 
R. Rhees Esq
100 Goldhurst Terrace
London N. W. 6
[Poststempel: BIRMINGHAM, 5 APR 1940]
Dear Rhees,
I got your letter here. I'll be back in Cambr. tonight. Was very glad to hear from you. I like the idea of your doing work in a factory. You'll get better & better I have no doubt, if you can stick. Best wishes to both of you. I hope to see you before long.
Wittgenstein
