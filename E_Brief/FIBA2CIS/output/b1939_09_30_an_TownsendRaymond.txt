Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Raymond Townsend, 30. 9. 1939

sourceDesc:  Original bei McGuinness; Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
As from Trinity College
Cambridge
30.9.39.
Dear Townsend,
This is only to tell you that I wrote to you about 2 weeks ago to the London address you gave me in your Letter from New Zealand. As soon as this reaches you, please, let me have a line from you.
Yours affectionately
Ludwig Wittgenstein
