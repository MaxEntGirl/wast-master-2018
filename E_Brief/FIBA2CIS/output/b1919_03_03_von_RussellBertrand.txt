Title: Von Bertrand Russell an Ludwig Wittgenstein, 3. 3. 1919

sourceDesc:  Original im BA. Publiziert (Cambridge Letters, S. 109; WiC, S. 87).    Austria Innsbruck Forschungsinstitut Brenner-Archiv 11/26.4    

Text: 
L. Wittgenstein
Leutnant i. d. Res.
(Prigionere di Guerra)
Cassino
Provincia Caserta
Italy
Manor House, Garsington, Oxford. 3.3.19.
Very glad to hear from you ‒ had been anxious for a long time. I shall be most interested to learn what you have done in Logic. I hope before long it may possible to hear all about it. Shall be glad of further news ‒ about your health etc.
B. Russell.
