Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, 22. 2. 1938

sourceDesc:  Abschrift (Cambridge Letters, S. 288). Publiziert (Cambridge Letters, S. 288f.; WiC, S. 266). 

Text: 
22 February 1938
Dear Moore,
I should be very grateful if you would let me know the result of the Council meeting regarding my MSS. My address is
36 Chelmsford Rd.,
Ranelagh, Dublin
I wish you'ld see Skinner one of these days.
Ever yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
