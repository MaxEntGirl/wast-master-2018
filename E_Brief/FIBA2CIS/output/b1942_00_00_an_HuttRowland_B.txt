Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Rowland Hutt, [1942 ?]

sourceDesc:  Original an der ÖNB (Autogr. 1280/31-11); Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
Nuffield House
Guy's Hospital
London S.E.I.
Dear Roland,
I was glad to hear from you. Your letter did not pain me; & I should like to see you if you'ld like to see me. ‒ I'm free every day after 4.30 or 5, on Saturdays after 12, &, of course, on Sundays. Let me know soon when you're coming.
So long! Good wishes!
Ludwig
