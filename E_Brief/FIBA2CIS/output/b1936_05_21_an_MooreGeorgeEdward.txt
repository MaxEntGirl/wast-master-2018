Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [21. 5. 1936]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 197; Cambridge Letters, S. 273). 

Text: 
Thursday
Dear Moore,
Mrs Thomson wants us, you & me, to come to tea to her to morrow. So I shan't come to you but meet you at the Thomsons (Lavender Cottage, 25 Storey's Way).
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
