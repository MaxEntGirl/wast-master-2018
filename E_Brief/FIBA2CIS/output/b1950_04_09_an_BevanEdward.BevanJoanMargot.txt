Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Edward Bevan und Joan Margot Bevan, [Ostern 1950 ?]

sourceDesc:  Aus einer Kopie in 'The Ray Monk Archive, The Random House Group Archive & Library, Rushden', Signatur: ‘Monk Ray, Wittgenstein Archive, Box 14’.  Abschrift und Kommentar Gabriel Citron, Scan in BA,  

Text: 
Sincere 
EASTER GREETINGS 
HAPPY DAYS 
Refreshed and sweet may 
Springtime flowers 
Enchant your eyes through 
Easter hours. 
And newborn hope light 
all your way 
To bring you gladness 
every day. 
To Doc & Mrs Bevan
from
Ludwig Wittgenstein B.M.
