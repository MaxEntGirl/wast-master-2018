Title: Von Bertrand Russell an Ludwig Wittgenstein, [vor 13. 12. 1919]

sourceDesc:  Original im BA. Publiziert (Cambridge Letters, S. 143; WiC, S. 112).    Austria Innsbruck Forschungsinstitut Brenner-Archiv 11/26.13    

Text: 
Hotel Twee Steeden
(Hotel des deux Villes)
Buitenhof
Dear Wittgenstein
This is my address. I have got here without misadventure, & I hope you will. Come on here straight the moment you arrive. It will be joy to see you again.
Yrs ever
B. R.
