Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Gilbert Pattisson, [14. 9. 1931]

sourceDesc:  Original an der Trinity College Library; Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
This is how the total eclipse looked in Mariazell. You see me soaring in the air. I shall come through London between the 5th & the 9th. I'll let you know the exact date later.
Bloodily
Ludwig
