[Poststempel: CAMBRIDGE, 6 OCT 1938]
Dear Rhees,
I'm in bed with a 'flu. I'm looking forward to seeing you! The University Press has accepted my book.
Best wishes!
So long!
Ludwig Wittgenstein.
Thanks for your letter. I see your point; but I still think you might have tried. Anyway I hope to see you soon
L. W.
