Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Roy Fouracre, [Weihnachten 1949]

sourceDesc:  Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
TO YOU
MY THOUGHTS
ARE TURNING
Now Christmastide is here,
Wishing that it may bring you
Whate'er your heart holds dear,
And all the best of Luck be yours,
For many a happy year.
To Roy
from
Ludwig
