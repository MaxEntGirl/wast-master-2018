Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [2. 6. 1936]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (Letters, S. 165; deutsch: Briefe, S. 197; Cambridge Letters, S. 276). 

Text: 
Tuesday
Dear Moore,
I am having a social gathering of my students in my rooms, on Friday at 4.30. Would you mind comming? If I don't hear from you I shall expect you.
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
