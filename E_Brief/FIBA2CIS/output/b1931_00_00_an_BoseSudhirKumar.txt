Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Sudhir Kumar Bose, [1931 ?]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift (von R. K. Gupta). 

Text: 
Magdalene College,
Cambridge
Dear Bose, I'ld like to see you before going down on Friday afternoon. Could you have lunch with me on Thursday about 1.30 at the Union? Let me know tomorrow if you can.
Yours
Wittgenstein
