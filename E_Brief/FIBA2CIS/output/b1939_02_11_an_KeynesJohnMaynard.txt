Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an John Maynard Keynes, 11. 2. 1939

sourceDesc:  Original an der King's College Library in Cambridge; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (Letters, S. 141; deutsch: Briefe, S. 215; Cambridge Letters, S. 309; WiC, S. 293). 

Text: 
81, East Rd.
Cambridge
11.2.39.
My dear Keynes,
Thanks for the telegram, & thanks for all the trouble you've gone to. I hope to God that you haven't made a mistake. I know, it's up to me to prove that you haven't. Well, I hope I'll be a decent prof.
Thanks again
Yours ever
Ludwig
