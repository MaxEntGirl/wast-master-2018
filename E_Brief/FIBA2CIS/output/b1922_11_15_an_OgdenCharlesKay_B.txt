Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Charles Kay Ogden, [nach 15. 11. 1922]

sourceDesc:  Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
Dear Ogden,
Please forward the enclosed letter to Russell. I've forgotten his address.
Best wishes. Yours sincerely
L. Wittgenstein
P.S. I've heard from Mrs Pinsent but not yet from Keynes.
