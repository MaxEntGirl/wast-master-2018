Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Alice Ambrose, [17. 6. 1933]

sourceDesc:  Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
Saturday
Dear Miss Ambrose,
Thanks so much for your invitation. Yes, I should like to come & dine with you. The day that would suit me best would be Thursday 22nd. If this is all right please let me know what time I should come.
Yours very sincerely
Ludwig Wittgenstein
P.S. I have no evening dress.
