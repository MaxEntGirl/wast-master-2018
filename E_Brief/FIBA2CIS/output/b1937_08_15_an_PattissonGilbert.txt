Text: 
Wishing
My
FRIEND
A Happy
BIRTHDAY
Birthday greetings
love sincere
Happy days be yours my dear,
From your loyal friend and true,
Speeds this greetings
card to you.
GOOD
LUCK
G. R. Pattisson Esq
42 Argyll Rd.
Kensington
London W.8.
[Poststempel: EXETER, 15 ? 1937]
I shall be at the pallace to-morrow, Friday, 6 p.m.. I hope you wont be busybodying in your office on Saturday.
bl...
Ludwig
Remember, even Bismarck could be replaced.
Keep this card till your [100th ?] birthday.
