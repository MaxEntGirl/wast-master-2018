Siglum: b1938_07_26_an_SraffaPiero

Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Piero Sraffa, [26. 7. 1938]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness, Kopie in BA. 

Text: 
81 East Rd 
Cambr. 
Tuesday 
Dear Sraffa,
I’ve seen Smythies & he can’t manage tomorrow evening. Would, say, next Monday at 9 p.m. do for you? Because he is free then. If I don’t hear from you to the contrary I’ll tell him to come to you on Monday.
So long!
Ludwig Wittgenstein
