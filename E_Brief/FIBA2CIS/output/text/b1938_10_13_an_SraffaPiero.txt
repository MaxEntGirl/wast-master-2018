Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Piero Sraffa, 13. 10. 1938

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness, Kopie in BA. 

Text: 
P. Sraffa Esq 
17B St Edward’s Passage 
Cambridge 
Dear Sraffa, I had a wire that I should come to Zurich tomorrow. So I’m off today. I expect I shall be back on Tuesday or Wednesday. I think things must be going pretty bad. 
So long! 
Ludwig Wittgenstein
