Siglum: b1942_09_10_an_RandRose

Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Rose Rand, 10. [9. 1942]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness. 

Text: 
Nuffield House 
Guy’s Hospital 
S. E. 1. 
Thursday 10th. 
 Dear Dr Rand,
I was glad to hear you had a job. I hope you’ll still have it when this reaches you. May a good spirit be with you in your work!
Yours sincerely 
Ludwig Wittgenstein
