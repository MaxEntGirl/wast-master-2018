Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, 5. 12. 1938

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 211; Cambridge Letters, S. 303). 

Text: 
81 East Rd
Cambridge
5.12.38.
Dear Moore,
I shall arrange times with my students at 3 p.m on Wednesday Jan. 18th in Wisdoms room at Trinity.
I should like to see you again some time this week if you don't mind. Would Friday at 4 or 5 be all right?
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
