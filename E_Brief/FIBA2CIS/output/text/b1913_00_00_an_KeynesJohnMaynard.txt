Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an John Maynard Keynes, [1913]

sourceDesc:  Original an der King's College Library in Cambridge; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 27; Cambridge Letters, S. 27). 

Text: 
Dear Keynes,
Would you mind having tea with me tomorrow? If you can come please don't trouble to reply.
Yrs
Ludwig Wittgenstein
