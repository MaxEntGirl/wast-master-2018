Siglum: b1939_07_24_an_SraffaPiero

Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Piero Sraffa, 24. 7. 1939

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness, Kopie in BA.  Publiziert (WiC, S. 306). 

Text: 
24.7.39
Dear Sraffa! This is only to say that things over here went very badly. I’m leaving tomorrow for Zurich where conferences continue. It’s as hot as hell here & reminds me in many other ways of that place too.
Ever yours 
Ludwig Wittgenstein 
My address: Poste Restante Zurich
