Title: Von Bertrand Russell an Ludwig Wittgenstein, 2. 3. 1919

sourceDesc:  Original im BA. Publiziert (Cambridge Letters, S. 108).    Austria Innsbruck Forschungsinstitut Brenner-Archiv 11/26.3    

Text: 
L. Wittgenstein (Prigionere di Guerra)
Leutnant i. d. Res.
Cassino
Provincia Caserta
Italy
Garsington Manor, near Oxford. 2.3.19
Most thankful to hear you are still alive. Please write on Logic when possible. I hope it will not be long now before a talk will be possible. I too have very much to say about philosophy etc.
B. Russell.
