Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Maurice O'Connor Drury, [1936]

sourceDesc:  nhaltsangabe (Drurys Notizen 1936, zit. nach Nedo, 1983, S. 283). 

Text: 
["He sent me as a birthday present a copy of Freud's Interpretation of Dreams. This, he wrote, was the most important of Freud's writings. When he first read it he said to himself, 'Here at last is a psychologist who has something to say.'"]
