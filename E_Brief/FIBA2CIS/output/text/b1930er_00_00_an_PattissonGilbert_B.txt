Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Gilbert Pattisson, [1930er]

sourceDesc:  Original an der Trinity College Library; Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
The Season's Greetings
BEST WISHES
From a treasured book
writ in words of gold,
Cherished memories from
its leaves unfold
Like the warm sunshine's
gladsome ray,
To make more happy your
Christmas Day.
(L. WITTGENSTEIN)
