Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Anna Rebni, 21. 8. 1946

sourceDesc:  Abschrift (Norweger Briefe, S. 158). Publiziert (Norweger Briefe, S. 158). 

Text: 
Frk. Anna Rebni 
Skjolden i Sogn 
Norway 
Dear Frk. Rebni,
Thank you for your letter which you gave Mr Richards for me. If you want to write to Marguerite Sjögren the best way is to write to her
c/o Hr Arvid Sjögren
15 Kornvægen
Stocksund
Sweden.
Arvid Sjögren is her brother in-law & will forward any post to her. He knows where she is at present.
All good wishes!
Yours sincerely
Ludwig Wittgenstein
