Dear Rhees,
I'm flying to Vienna today. Thanks for your kind letter. I'm in a hurry, feeling stupid & tired, so I'll just say that I'm thinking of you with the old feelings.
Ludwig Wittgenstein
I intend to stay in Vienna 4 weeks & then stay in England for a while dictating a M.S. Forgive this queer letter.
