Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Rose Rand, 30. 3. [1943]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness. 

Text: 
Nuffield House 
Guy’s Hospital 
S. E. 1. 
30.3. 
 Dear Miss Rand,
I am not certain whether I shall be able to lecture next term, or not.
 If I lecture you can, as last term, come to the lectures without applying for permission. If I can’t lecture I will let you know.
Yours sincerely 
L. Wittgenstein
