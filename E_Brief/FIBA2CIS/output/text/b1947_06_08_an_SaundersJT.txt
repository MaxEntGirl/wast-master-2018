Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an J. T. Saunders, 8. 6. 1947

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness.  Publiziert (WiC, S. 412). 

Text: 
Trinity College 
Cambridge 
8.6.47 
Dear Saunders,
I wish to apply for Sabbathical Leave for the Michaelmas Term 1947. If it is granted I propose to devote it to research.
Yours sincerely, 
L. Wittgenstein
