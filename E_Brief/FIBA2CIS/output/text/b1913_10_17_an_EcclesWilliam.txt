Siglum: b1913_10_17_an_EcclesWilliam

Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an William Eccles, [nach 17. 10. 1913]

sourceDesc:  Kopie im BA (Nedo, 1983, S. 106). 

Text: 
Dear Eccles,
Enclosed please find a Check for 23 Norwegian Kroner = ~ 23 s. & a bill from Goodalls. You would highly oblige me by paying the amount to Goodalls.
Yours ever
L.W.
Remember me to Mrs. Eccles.
