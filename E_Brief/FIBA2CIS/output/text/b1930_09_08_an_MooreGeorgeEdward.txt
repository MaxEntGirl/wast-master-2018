Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [8. 9. 1930]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift (Cambridge Letters, S. 248). Publiziert (Cambridge Letters, S. 248). 

Text: 
[Poststempel: WIEN, 8.9.30]
Dear Moore,
We have glorious weather since about a fortnight and my work is getting on rather well. This is another view of the place where I live. I hope you're having a good time too.
Yours ever,
Ludwig Wittgenstein
