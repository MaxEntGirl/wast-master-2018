Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [nach 18. 2. 1947 ?]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 226; Cambridge Letters, S. 329). 

Text: 
TRINITY COLL. CAMB.
Wednesday
Dear Moore,
I'm sorry to say, I'm in bed again; this time with something that looks like a gastric 'flu. So tomorrow's off again. An evil star or something seems to preside over our meetings.
I hope to see you before long, star or no star.
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
