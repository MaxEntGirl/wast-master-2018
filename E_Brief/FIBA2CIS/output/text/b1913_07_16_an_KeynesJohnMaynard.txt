Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an John Maynard Keynes, 16. 7. 1913

sourceDesc:  Original an der King's College Library in Cambridge; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (Letters, S. 109; deutsch: Briefe, S. 29; Cambridge Letters, S. 32; WiC, S. 41). 

Text: 
16.7.13.
HOCHREIT
POST HOHENBERG
N.-Ö.
Austria
Dear Keynes,
Thanks very much for the trouble you take over my business. ‒ My reason for not seeing you oftener last term was, that I did not wish our intercourse to continue without any sign that you wished to continue it.
Yours sincerely
Ludwig Wittgenstein
