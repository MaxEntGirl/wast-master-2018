Siglum: b1939_04_05_an_SraffaPiero

Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Piero Sraffa, [5. 4. 1939]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness, Kopie in BA. 

Text: 
Had a post card from my friend, the teacher, saying that everything went as well as possible & that they all are overjoied. Also got my certificate today. Good wishes.
Wittgenstein
