Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [13. 5. 1935]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 188; Cambridge Letters, S. 258). 

Text: 
Monday
Dear Moore,
I'm not going to send Miss Ambrose to you this afternoon. On second thought I dont think it would be the right thing. If you will allow me I shall see you tomorrow.
Yours ever
Ludwig Wittgenstein
