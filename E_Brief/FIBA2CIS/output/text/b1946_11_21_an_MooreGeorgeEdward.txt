Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, 21. 11. [1946 ?]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 224). 

Text: 
Trin. Coll.
Camb.
21.11.
Dear Moore,
I'm afraid I find that I have to go to London this morning on urgent business; so I won't be able to see you this afternoon. I'm very sorry. Perhaps you'll allow me to see you today week?
I'm sorry I wasn't able to notify you sooner.
So long! 
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
