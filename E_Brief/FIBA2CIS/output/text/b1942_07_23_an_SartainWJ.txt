Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an W. J. Sartain, 23. 7. 1942

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness.  Publiziert (WiC, S. 353). 

Text: 
Guy’s Hospital 
S.E.I. 
23.7.42. 
Dear Sartain,
 Thank you very much for your letter dated July 22nd. I resumed my work at Guy’s on July 9th.
 Yours sincerely, 
L. Wittgenstein
