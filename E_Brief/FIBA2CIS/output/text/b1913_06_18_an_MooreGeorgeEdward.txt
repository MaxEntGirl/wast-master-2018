Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, 18. 6. 1913

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 28; Cambridge Letters, S. 30). 

Text: 
SAVOY HOTEL, LONDON.
18.6 1913
Dear Moore,
My Mother stays in the Savoy Hotel. So we shall expect you there on Friday at about 1 p.m.
yours etc.
L Wittgenstein
