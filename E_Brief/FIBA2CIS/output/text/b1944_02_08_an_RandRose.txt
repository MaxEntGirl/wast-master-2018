Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Rose Rand, 8. 2. [1944]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness. 

Text: 
Clin. Research Lab. 
R. V. I. Newcastle
Dear Miss Rand,
you can always give my name as reference if you wish to; but there is very little I can write that will help you.
I hope you are fairly well & behaving moderately sensibly.
Yours sincerely 
L. Wittgenstein
