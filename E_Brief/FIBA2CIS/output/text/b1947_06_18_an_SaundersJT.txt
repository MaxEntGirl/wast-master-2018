Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an J. T. Saunders, 18. 6. 1947

sourceDesc:  Abschrift McGuinness.  Publiziert (WiC, S. 412). 

Text: 
TRIN. COLL. CAMB. 
18.6.47 
Dear Saunders,
Thank you for your letter of June 14th. I am very grateful to the General Board for granting me Sabbatical Leave.
Yours sincerely 
L. Wittgenstein
