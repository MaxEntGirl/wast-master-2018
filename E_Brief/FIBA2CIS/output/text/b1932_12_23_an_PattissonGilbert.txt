Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Gilbert Pattisson, [23. 12. 1932]

sourceDesc:  Original an der Trinity College Library; Kopie im BA. 

Text: 
G. R. Pattisson Esq
42 Argyll Road
Kensington
London W.8.
England
[Poststempel: WIEN, 23.XII.1932]
Frohe Weihnachten!
Motto: Nun ihr Musen genug!
If I don't hear from you to the contrary I expect you to meet me in Dover on Saturday 7th Jan. 1933 (this is important) I shall go via Ostende & consequently arrive in a slightly bl.... state.
Ludwig
Please write to me in any case.
