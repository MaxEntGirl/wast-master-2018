Title: Von Bertrand Russell an Ludwig Wittgenstein, [vor 13. 12. 1919]

sourceDesc:  Original im BA. Publiziert (Cambridge Letters, S. 144).    Austria Innsbruck Forschungsinstitut Brenner-Archiv 11/26.14    

Text: 
Hotel Twee Steden
(= des deux Villes)
Buitenhof, Den Haag
Dear Wittgenstein
This is my address ‒ Come here as quick as you can after your arrival in The Hague ‒ I am impatient to see you ‒ We will find some way to get your book published ‒ in England if necessary.
Yrs ever
B. R.
