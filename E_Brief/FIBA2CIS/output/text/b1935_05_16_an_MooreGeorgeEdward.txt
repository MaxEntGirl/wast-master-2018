Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, 16. 5. 1935

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 188; Cambridge Letters, S. 259; WiC, S. 242). 

Text: 
Trinity College
Cambridge
16th May 1935
Dear Moore,
This is only to confirm the statement I made in our conversation last Tuesday that I am resigning as Miss Ambrose's examiner for her Ph.D. degree.
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
