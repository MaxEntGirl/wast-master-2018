Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an John Maynard Keynes, [vor 25. 10. 1931]

sourceDesc:  Original an der King's College Library in Cambridge; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 181; Cambridge Letters, S. 252). 

Text: 
Dear Keynes,
I'm so sorry I cann't see you at tea time on Friday as I've got a class every Friday at 5 o/c. Could you have tea with me on Sunday 25th, or Saturday next 31st, or Sunday 1st November? I hope to see you soon
Yours ever
Ludwig
