Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an John Maynard Keynes, 8. 2. 1939

sourceDesc:  Original an der King's College Library in Cambridge; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (Letters, S. 140; deutsch: Briefe, S. 214; Cambridge Letters, S. 308; WiC, S. 292). 

Text: 
81, East Rd
Cambridge
8.2.39.
Dear Keynes,
Thanks for your kind note. Yes, the translation is pretty awful, & yet the man who did it is an excellent man. Only he's not a born translator, & nothing's more difficult to translate than colloquial (non-technical) prose.
Yours ever
Ludwig
