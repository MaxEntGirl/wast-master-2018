Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an John Maynard Keynes, [nach 3. 12. 1928]

sourceDesc:  Original an der King's College Library in Cambridge; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (Letters, S. 127; deutsch: Briefe, S. 166; Cambridge Letters, S. 226). 

Text: 
III. Kundmanngasse 19 Wien
My dear Keynes,
I had to postpone my trip, as my health was not quite strong enough in the first days of this month. But I am nearly well now & want to come to England in the beginning of January. Please write a line letting me know if I can see you then.
Yours ever
Ludwig
