Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [vor 25. 4. 1938]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 209; Cambridge Letters, S. 296). 

Text: 
Dear Moore,
In case you see any pupils on or before Monday who might be interested in my classes, would you mind telling them that I'll have the first meeting on Monday (25th) at 5 p.m.? We shall meet in Taylor's rooms in Trinity.
Ludwig Wittgenstein
