Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an Anna Rebni, [21. 12. 1946]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift (Norweger Briefe, S. 159). Publiziert (Norweger Briefe, S. 159). 

Text: 
Frk. Anna Rebni
Skjolden
i Sogn
Norge
Norway
[Poststempel: 21.12.1946]
Lots of love and gladness
Be yours this Christmas Day.
Good Luck with every blessing;
Attend your path alway.
With
Best Wishes
for a very happy
CHRISTMAS
and
New Year
To. Frk Anna Rebni
from
Ludwig Wittgenstein.
