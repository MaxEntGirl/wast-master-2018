Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [6. 3. 1936]

sourceDesc:  Original bei Timothy Moore; Kopie im BA. Publiziert (deutsch: Briefe, S. 196; Cambridge Letters, S. 273). 

Text: 
Friday
Dear Moore,
I'm sorry to say I can't see you today as I've got a bl.... cold & must stay indoors.
I hope to see you next Friday.
Yours
Ludwig Wittgenstein
