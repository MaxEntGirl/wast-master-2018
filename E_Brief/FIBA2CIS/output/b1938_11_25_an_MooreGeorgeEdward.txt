Title: Von Ludwig Wittgenstein an George Edward Moore, [25. 11. 1938]

sourceDesc:  Abschrift (Cambridge Letters, S. 302). Publiziert (Cambridge Letters, S. 302). 

Text: 
[Poststempel: HASTINGS, 25.11.1938]
Dear Moore,
We have been staying at 3 Pelham Crescent for ten days and have found it very satisfactory. I'm feeling better. I'll be back in Cambridge Monday. The weather is moderate.
Good wishes!
Ludwig Wittgenstein
Best wishes.
F. Skinner
